HIIT Area

High Intensity Interval Training area equipped with battle ropes, plyo boxes, and kettlebells to push your limits.

Maximum Results in Minimum Time

HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) is scientifically proven to be one of the most effective forms of exercise for fat loss and cardiovascular conditioning. Our dedicated HIIT zone gives you the space and tools to go all out.

Short, explosive intervals followed by brief rest periods keeps your heart rate elevated and your metabolism firing long after the workout ends — the famous "afterburn effect." Just 30 minutes of HIIT can outperform an hour of steady-state cardio.

  • Battle Ropes & Kettlebells
  • Plyometric Boxes
  • Open Turf for Sprints & Drills
  • Assault & Air Bikes
  • Coached HIIT Group Sessions

Why HIIT?

The science backs it up. Here's why thousands choose HIIT for maximum results.

Burns More Fat

HIIT keeps your metabolism elevated for up to 24 hours after your workout — burning more calories even at rest.

Time Efficient

Get an effective, complete workout in 20–30 minutes. Ideal for busy professionals who want maximum results.

Improves Fitness Fast

Regular HIIT training dramatically improves VO2 max, heart health, and endurance in as little as 2–3 weeks.
